Mochi Recipe

Ingredients:

- 1 cup mochiko (sweet rice flour)
- 1 cup water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- Cornstarch (for dusting)

Instructions:
1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine mochiko, water, and sugar. Mix well until smooth.
2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, leaving a small vent for steam to escape.
3. Microwave the mixture on high for 2 minutes. Stir the mixture, then cover and microwave for another 1-2 minutes until the mochi is thick and slightly translucent.
4. Dust a clean surface with cornstarch. Transfer the mochi onto the surface and let it cool slightly.
5. Once cool enough to handle, dust your hands with cornstarch and divide the mochi into small portions. Shape each portion into a ball or flatten it for filling.
6. Optionally, you can fill the mochi with sweet red bean paste or other fillings of your choice.
7. Serve and enjoy your homemade mochi!
